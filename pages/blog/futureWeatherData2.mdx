## Testing genereted weather data for future dates
In this video I go over the process to generate future weather data with a weather generating tool. This experiment is referencing an idea from and article [in the NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2011/05/23/science/earth/23adaptation.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0){:target="_blank"} that Chicago in the future will be similar in climate to the current day Baton Rouge LA. With this weather generating model and Ecotect’s Weather Tool I am able to compare the future weather data from 2050 to current weather data, pretty cool stuff…not the warming part but.
